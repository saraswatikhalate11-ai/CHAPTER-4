Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website so it appears higher in search engine results on platforms like Google. It involves using the right keywords, writing clear and useful content, optimizing page titles and descriptions, and making sure the website loads fast and works well on mobile devices. SEO also includes getting links from other trusted websites, which increases credibility. The main goal of SEO is to bring more organic (free) traffic to a website, helping businesses or individuals reach more people online without paying for ads.
